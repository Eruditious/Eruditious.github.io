This is my Impossible List

Many people have bucket lists, but few have impossible lists. I got the idea after reading this article and after reading the original creator's description (found here), I decided I would make my own impossible list.

Most people overestimate what they can do in one year and underestimate what they can do in ten years.

- Bill Gates


My personal philosophy is that each of us are given a lifetime to do whatever it is our heart tells us we need to do, experience as much of what we can that life has to offer, and to try and make a positive impact on the rest of the world. I view this list as a tentative, ever-growing, and above all else - a personal guide to how I plan on living the way I see that life should be lived.
